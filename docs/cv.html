Professional Summary

Final-year PhD candidate at UCL’s Social Research Institute with 4+ years’ experience evaluating social policies using advanced quantitative methods, administrative data, and mixed methods approaches. Expert in causal inference, quasi-experimental design, and secure data management across ONS and UCL platforms.
